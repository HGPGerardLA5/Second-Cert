Born September 19, 1993, raised in Columbia, South Carolina, yet having Belizean background, Jordan T. Jenks had an early interest in East Coast hip hop, and his first beats where made on FL studio. After dropping out Winthrop University, through his uncle's encouragement, Jenks moved to Atlanta to study sound engineering at SAE Institute. 2015, Jordan Jenks began working for Epic Records, but quit just a year later to produce for his career. Around that time, he produced for now well-known artists Trippie Redd and Young Nudy. But that wasn't what rose him to fame...

By February 2017, he met Playboi Carti, and collaborated on 2 tracks with him: Magnolia and wokeuplikethis*. Pi'erre also contributed to most tracks on Carti's debut album "Playboi Carti". After the album's release, on April 14th 2017, it would be Magnolia that rose both Playboi Carti and Pi'erre bourne to fame, as the track peaked #29 on US Billboard Hot 100. Pretty soon, Pi'erre was on his journey to fame from then on, as he was invited to with many more artists, most notably Trippie Redd, Young Thug, Lil Yachty, and Lil Uzi Vert.

üë• COLLABORATIONS üë•

Pi'erre Bourne is mostly known for collaborating with Playboi Carti having produced 6 songs on his self-titied/debut album, with hits wokeuplikethis and Magnolia leading their fame in the rap industry. Alongside with Carti's debut album, he also executively produced Carti's sophomore album Die Lit having produced 15 out of the 19 songs that appeared on the album. Popular songs that appeared on the album include Flatbed Freestyle, RIP, Poke It Out, etc. Travis Scott's song "Watch" which featured Kanye West and Lil Uzi Vert was produced by Pi'erre Bourne, so was "Ye" and "Jesus is King" album by Kanye. May 8th, 2019, Pi'erre Bourne, collaborated with Young Nudy to produce "Sli'merre", which consists of 12 songs whcih features DaBaby, Megan Thee Stallion, and 21-Savage, alongisde Lil Uzi Vert. The album charted #1 on Apple's music US All Genres chart, and debuted #167 on US Billboard Top 200. Pi'erre Bourne's production credits on "Eternal Atake" by Lil Uzi Vert got him a spot at number 1 on Billboard's Hot Top 100 producers. By May 1 2020, Dark Lane Demo Tape's own track "Pain1993" by Drake featuring Playboi Carti was produced by Pi'erre Bourne. The track peaked at number 7 on Top 100 not too long after.

WHAT ABOUT HIMSELF? ü§î

Pi'erre Bourne recognized his own artistry and decided to also create his own songs, not just make beats for others. So he released his own album series The Life of Pi'erre which gained immenent fame with the release of #4 in the series. "The Life of Pi'erre 4" released June 21 2019, and nearly a year later, a deluxe version released alongside. This album gained traction for Pi'erre alongside the Die Lit album(which he produced) that released a year prior. 2 years after the released of #4, "The Life of Pi'erre 5" released, which has been Pi'erre biggest albums yet. Notable songs on the album include Drunk n Nasty, 4U, Sossboy 2, and Groceries, which coincedentally are also released as singles. Other albums released include "Good Movie", released September 2, 2022, "GRAILS", which is an EP, released April 14, 2023, and an upcoming "Made in Paris" album to be released sometime in 2024.
